🎓 Aetram Groups Website - Case Study

Role: UI/UX Designer cum Developer – Responsible for layout design, structure, visual consistency, and part of the development team.

🔍 Overview
  • Client: Aetram Group of Companies
  • Industry: Financial Services, Trading, IT & Corporate Services
  • Platform: Responsive website
  • Project Type: Corporate website with service listing and regional presence
  • Contribution: Designed and developed key layouts and pages (Home, Services, Bullion, and Loader screens)
👁️ Problem Statement

Aetram Group needed a digital presence that reflected their professional, high-value services across global regions (UAE, India, Mauritius, etc.). The challenge was to design a site that feels premium, informative, and credible.

✏️ My Contribution

I was involved in both designing and implementing:

  • Landing Page with Loader screen and brand logo animation
  • Home Page layout with structured content (Intro, services, licensed regions)
  • Bullion Page with a clean product overview layout
  • Page responsiveness and visual alignment across devices
  • Ensured UI consistency with dark-gold color theme
  • Worked with development team for real-time testing and bug fixes
🌟 Goals
  • Create a modern, responsive design
  • Clearly showcase services and regions
  • Convey trust through visual tone (dark theme + gold)
  • Ensure fast load and smooth transitions
👨‍👧‍👦 Target Audience
  • Business clients
  • Investors
  • Government & Corporate Partners
  • High-net-worth individuals
🎨 Tools Used
  • Figma: Wireframe layout & UI structure
  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap 5
  • Responsive Testing Tools: Cross-device design checks
🔍 UX Process
1. Content Mapping

Gathered brand offerings and organized them into categories:

  • Broking
  • IT Solutions
  • Bullion
  • HR and Corporate Services
2. Wireframing

Structured content flow ensuring readability and hierarchy:

  • Hero section with strong headline and brand tone
  • Services grid with icon-based layout for scalability
  • Bullion page split into product categories for clarity
3. Visual Design
  • Gold and black palette to evoke trust and luxury
  • Iconography used for clear communication of services
  • Real gold and crystal imagery to emphasize value
📊 Impact

Delivered a visually cohesive, professional site.
Structured layout improved readability and navigation.
Professional branding tone attracted high-level leads.

✨ Key Learnings

Consistent UI elements increase brand trust.
Dark backgrounds require high contrast for readability.
Collaboration between design and development teams enhances final product quality.
